Leave of the CS

← Back

Event Overview

Our youngest ever staff member, yet alone executive staff member - Mr. Thabiso Morupisi-Khan - as the Chief of Staff (CS) is taking a year's long leave from his duties in Oratory League to focus on the completion of his 12th grade studies. This is for him to idealy prepare him for his final secondary education examination dubbed the BSSE (Botswana Secondary School Examination). Our President - Mr. Leatile King Baaitse - wished him a heartfelt token of great luck and we look forward to his return. Ergo, there will be in acting Chief of Staff (CS) in his place temporarily to allow Mr. Khan's absence from greatly affecting the organization's operations More Pressure | More Precious!.
