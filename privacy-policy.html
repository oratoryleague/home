Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Oratory League. We are committed to protecting your personal information and preserving your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with our website and services. Our practices are guided by our core values of Integrity, Unison, and Elegance.

By accessing or using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy. We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time, so please review it regularly.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

You may voluntarily provide us with personal information, which may include:

  • Name, email address, telephone number, and mailing address
  • Demographic details (such as age, gender, and location)
  • Payment and financial details when processing memberships or registrations

2.2 Non-Personal Information

We also collect non-personal data that does not directly identify you. This may include:

  • Technical information such as browser type, operating system, and IP address
  • Usage data regarding your interactions with our website and services
  • Information collected through cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies

3. Use of Your Information

Your information is used to enhance your experience with Oratory League, including:

  • Processing and managing memberships and registrations
  • Sending updates, newsletters, and event information
  • Customizing website content based on your interests
  • Conducting research and analytics to improve our services
  • Ensuring compliance with legal and regulatory requirements

4. Disclosure of Information

We may share your information under the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: Trusted third parties assist us with website operations, communication, and transactions. These providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • For Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law or to comply with a valid legal request.
  • In Business Transactions: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred as part of the transaction.

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. While we strive to safeguard your data, no system can guarantee complete security.

6. Your Rights

Based on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data. These may include:

  • Access to your personal information
  • Requesting corrections or updates
  • Requesting deletion of your data
  • Restricting or objecting to data processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the details provided below.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and analyze usage patterns. You may adjust your cookie settings via your browser; however, disabling cookies may affect certain functionalities.

8. Third-Party Services

Our website may include links to third-party sites or services not operated by Oratory League. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies before providing personal information.

9. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent. If you believe such data has been inadvertently collected, please contact us immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred and maintained on servers outside your country, where data protection laws may differ. By using our services, you consent to these transfers.

11. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law. When no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize your data.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Oratory League reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: oratoryleague@gmail.com
Address: Gaborone, Botswana

14. Additional Information

At Oratory League, we value the trust you place in us. Our privacy practices reflect our commitment to transparency, accountability, and excellence. We continually update our policies to adapt to new technologies and legal requirements.

Thank you for being a valued member of our community.
