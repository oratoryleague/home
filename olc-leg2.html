About OLC Leg 2

The OLC Leg 2 event is the Southern Regional Round of the Oratory League Championships, offering an unparalleled platform for aspiring debaters to showcase their skills and engage with peers from various regions. Join us for a day of thrilling debates, networking, and educational opportunities.

Event Highlights

  • Regional debates featuring top talents.
  • Workshops conducted by expert mentors.
  • Opportunities to network with fellow debaters and industry professionals.
  • Awards and recognition for exceptional performances.

Event Details

Date: July 15, 2025

Venue: Southern Regional Auditorium, Gaborone

Time: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
