The Grand Orate

Be a part of the transformative Grand Orate as we strive to reignite the spirit, talent and passion for oratory with scholars from Botswana, South Africa, Lesotho, Malawi, Namibia, Zambia, Tanzania & Sweden. Categories of the championship will be debate, public speaking, quiz, poetry, moot court, theatre & spelling bee. Make sure to register now to get to engage with our incredible theme of Power, Progress & Ethics of Control this December. Stay in tune with our updates for venue and date announcements.
