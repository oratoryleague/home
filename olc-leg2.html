About OLC Leg 2

The OLC Leg 2, short for Oratory League Championship Leg 2, is an event for the Southern Regional Round of the Oratory League Championships, offering an unparalleled platform for aspiring debaters, public speakers, mooters, spellers and other orators across the diverse spectrum, to showcase their skills and engage with peers from various regions. Join us for a day of thrilling debates, networking, and educational opportunities.

Event Highlights

  • Regional debates and orates featuring top talents.
  • Workshops conducted by expert mentors.
  • Opportunities to network with fellow orators and industry professionals.
  • Awards and recognition for exceptional performances.
