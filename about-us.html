Our Mission

At Oratory League, our mission is to empower orators worldwide through education, competition, and global networking. We strive to advance debate and oratory into legislative literature, fostering a community of skilled and confident speakers.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create an elegant, professional, and user-centric platform that facilitates debate education, competition management, global outreach, and career building in oratory and debate. We aim to reflect our journey of excellence, focus on innovation, and commitment to empowering debaters worldwide.

Our History

Co-founded in November 2024, by Asante Aupa Modala and Leatile King Baaitse, Oratory League began as an initiative and a coalition of young orators striving to participate in oratory championships. However, ever since then, it has grown to become an oratory organization making strides in education and orate judiciary.
