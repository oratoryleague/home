Event Overview

The 2024 Orate Africa Championships was an annual event held and created by the Orate Africa Trust. The 2024 version was dubbed "Homecoming" as a tribute to Botswana being the birthplace of the organization. It had multiple Southern African countries participating, namely Botswana, Lesotho, Namibia and Zimbabwe. The categories for the competition included debate, public speaking, quiz and moot court.

Event Highlights

Orate Competition

Schools participated in 7 preleminary rounds, with the Spelling Bee team (Gorutwa L. Sololo, Lapologang Motiki & Duduetsang Selemogo) making it to the finals, finishing as the 3rd runner-up and waving the Oratory League flag high!

Networking

Opportunities to network with fellow orators and industry professionals. Scholars from across the country and Southern African region got to share ideas and cultures.

Forging Memories

It was an incredible opportunity to kindle bonds among delegates. The Oratory League Team had the priceless opportunity to reunite and forge incredible memories together, forever strengthening the love and bond between them.
