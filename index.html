Our Inaugural OLC

Be a part of the transformative Oratory League Championships (OLC) Leg 1 as we strive to reignite the spirit, talent and passion for oratory in the northeast, northwest & central regions. Make sure to register now to get to engage with our incredible theme of Power, Progress & Ethics of Control at Masunga SSS from 16th - 23rd April 2025.
