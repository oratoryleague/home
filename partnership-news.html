About HMCP

Heavenly Mum Care Packages (HMCP) is a women-led healthcare company founded by a young mother who experienced firsthand the challenges of postpartum recovery. HMCP addresses these challenges by offering essential healing products tailored to different birthing procedures, packaged into convenient and affordable postpartum care kits. Since its inception in 2024, HMCP has made significant strides in empowering mothers by providing not only physical support but also emotional care through a platform that connects new mothers with clinical therapists and gynecologists.

Their dedication to making motherhood smoother has earned them recognition in various entrepreneurship and innovation circles, including accolades such as the Forbes30Under30 Pitch Competition Semi-Finalists and Global Startup Awards Regional Finalists. We are proud to be part of the movement that is reshaping how postpartum care is perceived and provided, helping mothers reclaim their health and confidence during the crucial period following childbirth.


Navigating Transformations Together: A Journey to Creating a Balance Between Motherhood and Success

Date: 1st March 2025



Event Overview: Navigating Transformations Together


Oratory League is one of the proud partners of HMCP. Ergo, we are excited to co-host their upcoming event dubbed Navigating Transformations Together. Our Assistant Finance Director - Ms. Angel Letshola - will be representing the League as a co-host of the incredible event.

The event will be at the BNYC Hall venue, designed to bring together 75 young mothers from diverse backgrounds and professions for an empowering, heart-to-heart conversation on balancing motherhood with personal and professional success. The event will feature open discussions on the personal and professional journeys of mothers who have navigated the complexities of motherhood and career life. It will be a safe space for sharing experiences, challenges, and victories, while providing practical tools and advice for achieving a healthy work-life balance.


Event Objectives and Expected Outcomes


  • • Networking and Empowerment: Foster a supportive community where mothers—from domestic workers and street vendors to C-suite leaders—can share insights and strategies on managing the delicate balance between work, home, and self-care.

  • • Heart-to-Heart Conversations: Provide a platform for authentic discussions where mothers exchange their real-life experiences, discuss challenges, and support one another.

  • • Practical Guidance: Ensure attendees leave with actionable tips, inspiration, and renewed confidence to better manage their lives, careers, and families.

  • • Creating a Lasting Impact: Redefine the narrative of motherhood by debunking myths and breaking down barriers—empowering women to pursue their dreams without guilt.


Target Audience


This event is designed for a diverse group of 75 mothers, including:

  • - Domestic Workers
  • - Street Vendors
  • - Small Business Owners
  • - Corporate Leaders (C-suite, middle management, etc.)
  • - Freelancers
  • - Stay-at-home Moms


Impact of the Event


This event will have a far-reaching impact on individual mothers, their families, workplaces, and communities. By equipping mothers with the tools, inspiration, and support needed to balance personal and professional lives, we aim to build stronger, more resilient communities. Empowered mothers can become transformative leaders, contributing to a broader societal shift toward more supportive, flexible, and inclusive work environments.


  • • Foster Stronger Connections: Build a network of mothers who support one another both professionally and personally.

  • • Inspire Confidence: Encourage mothers to embrace their dual roles as caregivers and professionals.

  • • Shape a New Narrative: Challenge and transform traditional views on motherhood and career success.
