Reporting Process

Oratory League maintains strict protocols for addressing misconduct. All reports are treated with utmost confidentiality and investigated by our independent Ethics Committee. Below you'll find our comprehensive reporting framework:

Primary Reporting

Email: oratoryleague@gmail.com
Carbon Copy (CC): OL Ethics Reporting System
WhatsApp: +267 75 726 916

Anonymous Options

Submit reports anonymously through our encrypted portal. All anonymous reports receive equal attention while protecting your identity through secure blockchain technology.

Digital Reporting Form

Accepted formats: PDF, JPG, MP4, DOC (Max 25MB)

Investigation Process

1. Report Receipt

Automatic confirmation within 24 hours with case reference number

2. Preliminary Review

Initial assessment completed within 3-5 business days

3. Full Investigation

Detailed examination by Ethics Committee (14-30 days)

4. Resolution

Final determination communicated to all involved parties

Confidentiality Protections

  • All reports encrypted using military-grade (AES-256) encryption
  • Strict need-to-know access protocol for investigation files
  • Whistleblower protection against retaliation
  • Data retention policy: 7 years post-case resolution
