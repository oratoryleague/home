Our Mission

At Oratory League, our mission is to empower orators worldwide through education, competition, and global networking. We strive to advance debate and oratory into legislative literature, fostering a community of skilled and confident speakers. We plan on elevating debate standards and empowering all members of the youth community. The Youth are the pivotal demographic of the economy, and as such, their talent must be nurtured. The power of speech can get you want. Oratory League aims at increasing the standards of orates and speaking skills for the members of the world economy. We embrace our principles of Unison, Elegance and Integrity!

Our Vision

Our vision is to create an elegant, professional, and user-centric platform that facilitates debate education, competition management, global outreach, and career building in oratory and debate. We aim to reflect our journey of excellence, focus on innovation, and commitment to empowering debaters worldwide. We envision a future where oratory shapes the economy in many constructs. Our organization plans on drawing up a comprehensive system of debate that is more efficient than the current judiciary system in solving and tackling legal cases. We give the world the liberation of choosing for their cases to be handled by either professional attorneys or professional debaters. It would mitigate the weight on the judicial processes, thus accelerating the rate at which the economy is served justice. In doing so, we are developing a new curriculum and course to install into universities and tertiary institutions, allowing passionate debaters and orators around the world economy to enroll, study and become professional debaters. We see it moot for candidates to win debate championships and not be able to study such a course, whereas those who partake in moot court makes them fit to be lawyers. Our well-narrated system allows debaters to handle cases on strict parameters, proofing to yield more fairness and justice than the status quo of world standards.

Our History

Co-founded in November 2024, by Asante Aupa Modala and Leatile King Baaitse, Oratory League began as an initiative and a coalition of young orators striving to participate in oratory championships. However, ever since then, it has grown to become an oratory organization making strides in education and orate judiciary.
