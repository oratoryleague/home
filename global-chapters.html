Global Chapters

Connecting orators across continents with local excellence

Our Worldwide Network

Oratory League's global chapters are the lifeblood of our community – uniting debaters, speakers, and thought leaders from Africa, Europe, Asia, North America, South America, and Oceania. Each chapter offers localized training, events, and support, fostering a rich exchange of ideas and empowering individuals to lead in their communities.

Africa Chapter

Dedicated to nurturing the continent's vibrant oratory tradition, our Africa Chapter offers regional training, local events, and a supportive network of emerging leaders.

Europe Chapter

Bringing together diverse cultures with a shared passion for debate, the Europe Chapter fosters international collaboration and excellence in public speaking.

Asia Chapter

Merging tradition with innovation, our Asia Chapter provides a dynamic platform for debate and public discourse, nurturing talent across rapidly evolving communities.

North America Chapter

Committed to professional development and community engagement, the North America Chapter offers premier training programs, competitions, and networking opportunities.

South America Chapter

Embracing the region's vibrant spirit, our South America Chapter creates a nurturing environment for emerging orators, hosting innovative events and competitions.

Oceania Chapter

Connecting local communities across Oceania, this chapter provides resources, training, and events that celebrate eloquence and foster regional leadership.
