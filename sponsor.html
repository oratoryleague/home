Your Sponsorship Transforms Lives!

Your sponsorship fuels Oratory League—a youth-driven force established in November 2024 dedicated to championing intellectual oratory, critical thinking, and leadership. With your support, our rising orators gain the platform to tackle Botswana’s most pressing challenges. From addressing the education crisis affecting over 11,570 students to combating the impacts of climate change and harnessing AI for personalized learning through partners like GreyEd, your contribution drives transformative change. Moreover, your sponsorship enables our vital work in HIV and health advocacy, ensuring that our youth become active contributors to policy discussions and social transformation. Together, we are not just advancing debate; we are shaping the future of Botswana and Africa.
