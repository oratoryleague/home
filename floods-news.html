Message from the League

We serve the communities of Gaborone and areas affected by the recent floods with our unwavering support. We pray and hope that the houses and infrastructures that have been damaged may be repaired and those that are unrepairable to be rebuilt, better. It is during times likes these that Botswana proves to be a nation of solidarity, ergo, we urge Batswana to continue helping each other and and supporting their friends, families, colleagues and community members to mitigate the effects of the floods. We extend our gratitude to all acts of selflessness and bravery in this crisis, those that have put great efforts in rescuing others and the government of Botswana in taking caution to keep Batswana safe and their efforts to spearhead rescue missions.

For the lives lost, we extend our unwavering support to the families and friends that are grieving the loss of their loved ones. We encourage them to embrace their souls as their legacies become etched in the annals of history and forever keep them close to our hearts. As the diamonds of Botswana, we will build back better and stronger.

From Oratory League to Botswana; MORE PRESSURE, MORE PRECIOUS! That is how diamonds are made.
